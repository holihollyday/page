Internet Art Slot Machine

Commision by The Neu Lithium

Much like a regular slot machine, the Internet Art Slot Machine has three slots, 1) a silent video clip; 2) a sound clip; and 3) a short paragraph of text. By clicking on “Shuffle!” you randomize the combination of a silent moving image, a sound clip, and a text, and you will view the final combination as one whole piece. The final result is an amalgamation of audiovisual information we get from Internet browsing.
